# Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Blog

Welcome to our blog dedicated to exploring the fascinating intersection of artificial intelligence and physical robotics. Here, we'll dive deep into the concepts of embodied intelligence and discuss how AI systems can operate effectively in the physical world.

## What is Physical AI?

Physical AI represents a paradigm shift from traditional digital AI toward embodied intelligence. Rather than treating AI as purely software-based systems, Physical AI emphasizes the importance of physical interaction with the environment as a fundamental aspect of intelligence.

In this blog series, we'll explore:

- How robots can learn through physical interaction
- The challenges of bridging digital AI with real-world robotics
- Practical implementations of AI in robotic systems
- Case studies of successful Physical AI implementations
- The latest research and developments in humanoid robotics

## Our Journey Through Physical AI

This blog accompanies our comprehensive curriculum on "Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics," where we'll cover:

- **ROS 2 Fundamentals** - Building the nervous system of robotics applications
