ğŸ¤– ğŸ§  Physical AI Principles ğŸ¤–â€‹

Physical AI represents a significant transition from AI models confined to digital environments to embodied intelligence that operates in physical space. This approach focuses on:

  • Understanding physical laws and their impact on AI systems
  • Developing embodied intelligence that interacts with the real world
  • Creating AI systems that function in reality rather than just simulations
  • Integrating real-world sensorimotor experiences into AI models

Our curriculum specifically emphasizes humanoid robotics because:

  • Humanoid robots excel in human-centered environments due to their shared physical form
  • They can be trained with abundant data from human interaction environments
  • They represent the pinnacle of embodied intelligence challenges
  • They require integration of multiple complex systems (locomotion, manipulation, perception)
